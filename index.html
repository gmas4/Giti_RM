Our Story

We keep it simple - bold flavors, clean ingredients, and zero compromises.

At RootsMatter, we set out with a simple belief—snacking can be both delicious and intentional. Inspired by time-honored recipes and modern nutrition, we carefully curate each snack to bring you the best of both worlds.

Our journey started with a passion for clean, wholesome ingredients that fuel the body without compromising on taste. Every product we create is a tribute to our love for real food, mindful living, and meaningful connections.

Join us in redefining snacking—one bite at a time.

Healthy snacks

Our range of snacks is:

  • Wholesome & Nutritious – Packed with natural goodness
  • Free from Artificial Additives – No preservatives, no fillers
  • Made with simple, clean ingredients - Pure flavours, clean food made for modern lifestyles.
  • Rooted in Community - Creating a ripple effect by fostering connections, supporting local initiatives, and inspiring mindful food choices for a more conscious world.

Organic staples

‘Shastra’ is a Sanskrit word which means specialized knowledge in a defined area of practice. In this context, our products stands for the pure, pristine and enriched goodness of organic food. In a burgeoning market of processed and chemicals laced foods, our staples holds the promise of imbuing in our lifestyles the purity of the true and all natural origin of food.

Our organic staples are sourced from trusted farmers and producers who share our commitment to sustainable practices. We believe in the power of organic farming to nourish not just our bodies, but also the planet.
